Our Principles

It’s no longer good enough to have the most important information about your business locked up in a laptop on an analyst’s spreadsheet.

We believe that creating real value from your business data requires dedication and discipline, and data and analytics teams can learn much from the world of DevOps and traditional software engineering.

Your business is best served when we follow these principles:

  • Understanding your data is in every employee's job description. We're here to help them do that.
  • There can be a single source of truth for every analytic question within a company.
  • Data quality is paramount and only through testable, repeatable, peer reviewed processes can you gain trust in your data.
  • We focus your limited resources where data will have the greatest impact.
  • We best serve the needs of your business with a high-quality, maintainable code base.
  • Every piece of business logic can and should be defined in code.
  • Every piece of analytics code can and should be version controlled.
  • Every piece of analytics code can and should pass a documented test.
